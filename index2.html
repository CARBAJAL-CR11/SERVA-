General Information

What is it?

It is a system that manages restaurant reservations, it can be used by employees and managers, its objective is to streamline the process.

Functionality

In order to ensure user comfort, the forms are optimized in terms of size and buttons, as well as the information displayed.

Main idea

Develop a system to solve different problems that may arise during the management of a reservation and make it easy and convenient.
